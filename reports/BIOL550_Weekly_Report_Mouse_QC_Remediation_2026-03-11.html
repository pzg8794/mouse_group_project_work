Problems encountered

As seen in the validation summary output on the left, the original FASTX-trimmed baseline was still not alignment-ready for the technical artifact modules we were trying to fix. The summary shows 52 Adapter Content FAIL reports, 2 Overrepresented sequences FAIL reports, and a median adapter_max of 31.8806 under FASTX. This is the evidence that the residual adapter-related signal was still present after the first trimming strategy.

The same validation summary also shows what changed after fastp and what did not. Adapter Content FAIL dropped from 52 to 0, Overrepresented sequences FAIL dropped from 2 to 0, and median adapter_max dropped from 31.8806 to 0.0051. However, not every FastQC flag changed after fastp: Per base sequence content remained split across 26 WARN and 26 FAIL, and Sequence Length Distribution remained 52 WARN. Based on the file-by-file review and the course guidance, we might treat these as signals to monitor rather than reasons to keep trimming, because they are consistent with RNA-seq composition effects and expected post-trim read-length variability.
